The Property

Where nature meets refined living

Rare in every sense, this Berkeley Hills estate spans two parcels totaling over 12,500 square feet — offering a lifestyle that is equal parts serene retreat and sophisticated entertainer's dream.

Beyond a private, understated entrance, a tranquil courtyard sets the tone. High ceilings, natural light, and sweeping Bay views unfold across a split-level layout that balances open gathering spaces with peaceful private retreats.

The chef's kitchen is the heart of the home: quartz countertops, custom cabinetry, premium appliances, and a wine fridge, opening seamlessly to the dining area and a generous deck where Bay views make every meal feel like an occasion.

Hardwood floors run throughout. A gas fireplace anchors the living room. The main level offers three bedrooms including a primary suite retreat and a flexible bedroom with private exterior entry — ideal as a home office or studio. Downstairs: a family room, media area, wet bar, full bath, and a guest suite opening directly to a private deck amid lush greenery.

A bocce court, fruit trees, dual laundry areas, wine storage, and a two-car garage with 220V EV outlet complete this extraordinary property.

The ADU — a home within a home

The renovated accessory dwelling unit is the property's most remarkable feature. With its own private entrance, deck, and sweeping Bay views, it opens possibilities that few Berkeley Hills properties can offer.

Whether for multigenerational living, rental income, a dedicated workspace, or a guest house — this is a rare asset that dramatically expands what the property can be.

As part of the sale, the seller would also like to include 1162 Miller, which contains the gorgeously remodeled ADU structure. Call the listing agents for more information.
